About Jelly Roll

Jelly Roll is an American music artist known for blending country, rock, rap, and soulful storytelling. His music often focuses on personal struggles, recovery, redemption, family, and real-life experiences. His emotional lyrics and unique sound have helped him connect with fans from many different music backgrounds.

This website highlights Jelly Roll's background, music, albums, and recent news. The goal of the site is to create a simple fan website that is easy to navigate and uses a consistent external style sheet across all three pages.
